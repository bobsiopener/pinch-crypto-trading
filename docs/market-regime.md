### Regime Classification Framework

We define 4 market regimes for crypto:

#### 1. 🟢 RISK-ON BULL
- BTC above 200-day MA, making higher highs
- Positive ETF flows, strong momentum
- Macro accommodative (rate cuts, QE)
- **Best strategies:** Momentum, trend following, buy dips
- **NOT current regime**

#### 2. 🔴 MACRO-DRIVEN BEAR ← **CURRENT**
- BTC below key MAs, correlated with equities
- Macro headwinds dominate (inflation, rates, geopolitics)
- Risk-off flows to gold, bonds, cash
- **Best strategies:** Macro swing (event-driven), selective shorts, heavy cash
- **Key:** Wait for the catalyst that shifts the regime (Fed pivot, conflict resolution)

#### 3. 🟡 CRYPTO-NATIVE BEAR
- Triggered by crypto-specific events (exchange collapse, regulation, protocol failure)
- BTC decorrelates from equities, falls on its own
- Contagion risk through DeFi/leverage
- **Best strategies:** Stay out. Pure cash until contagion clears.
- **NOT current regime** (no crypto-native catalyst)

#### 4. ⚪ SIDEWAYS / CONSOLIDATION
- BTC range-bound for 4+ weeks
- Low volatility, declining volume
- No clear macro catalyst
- **Best strategies:** Grid trading, mean reversion, DCA
- **NOT current regime** (volatility too high)

### Regime Transition Signals

**What would shift us to RISK-ON BULL:**
- Fed announces rate cuts (or signals imminent cuts)
- Iran conflict de-escalates, oil drops below $75
- BTC reclaims 200-day MA with volume
- ETF inflows resume for 5+ consecutive days
- Operation Latinum Rush hits 5/7 green signals

**What would shift us to CRYPTO-NATIVE BEAR:**
- Major exchange insolvency
- Tether/USDT depeg
- US crypto regulation shock (ban, punitive tax)
- Major DeFi protocol exploit (>$1B)

**What would shift us to SIDEWAYS:**
- Macro catalysts exhaust without resolution
- BTC establishes a tight range (e.g., $65-72K for 4+ weeks)
- Volatility declines, VIX normalizes below 18

### Strategy Implications for Current Regime

1. **Default stance: Heavy cash (50-70% of account)**
2. **Selective macro swing trades** around CPI, FOMC, NFP
3. **Asymmetric bets** — risk 1, target 2-3 (don't need to be right most of the time)
4. **Quick exits** — don't fight the trend; take profits early
5. **Monitor regime shift signals daily** — the opportunity is catching the turn
